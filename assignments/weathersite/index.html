Weather Story

Lots of interesting things happen here as far as weather goes. We get sunshine and storms. Storms can produce rain, hail, and even snow. It rains in the summer, spring, and fall. It snows in the fall, winter, and spring. Summers bring heat, but there is usually a nice breeze that helps to keep our temperatures milder than the surrounding areas.
