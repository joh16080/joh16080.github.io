Cycle the Continental Divide

60K Trail Race

7:30 AM on August 4, 2018

Explore the gorgeous trails along the Bridger Range. The race begins on the North Coottonwood Creek trail which links with the Ridge Trail that will take you to expansive views of Bozeman and the surrounding area. The race will continue on to the College 'M' Trail which will then link up with the Foothills trail that will lead you along the side of the range. Finally, meeting up with the North Cottonwood Creek trail and the exciting finish line!
