Daily Tours

Cherry River

This 1+ mile trail is great for kids, birdwatching, and has beautiful views. The trail is mostly flat and will give kids and beginner mountain bikers the experience of biking on trails with limited risks.

Highland Glen Nature Preserve

This is a 5 mile trail with rolling hills and active wildlife that will keep you engaged. This trail is open throughout all the seasons and is different every time you visit.

Triple Tree Trail

This is a 5+ mile trail with steep hills that contains a reward of gorgeous views. This is a local favorite and is well taken care of. This trail will take you to great heights while also having portions of forest shade to enjoy.

Leverich Canyon

This is a 6+ mile trail of amazing trails that are a bikers dreamland. The trail is a climb to the ridgetop that will take your breath away. Forests, meadows, and creeks are a feast for your eyes. The miles speed away as you engage with nature.
